Introduction

World's most trusted Sabhy Certification Body for assurance of trust in every Bussiness. Achieving excellence in issuing Sabhy compliance certification , Independent Auditing & Monitoring systems and promoting other acceptable products and services.

Our Vision

To create such a methodology for our Indian society in which by adopting the lifestyle suggested by our Indian sages, a peaceful and healthy society can be developed by reducing the errors of today’s era in which the lost traditions and styles will be preserved. Hence, Restoration of our Society is possible.

Objective

To create such a simple process through which individually if a person wants too he can make a difference , a change on his own in the revival of society, culture and health in a very easy way for which he can get the help he needs from us by following our certification process . Therefore he will get a feeling of satisfaction in being able to do something for the Country as well as helping in building a stronger and united Society.

Classification

Our country India has been full of different beliefs, traditions and diversity for thousands of years. For the world that has an understanding of the country and society, there are some basic sentiments to keep India united despite India's diversity, which is based on the underlying sentiments of living and thinking for centuries. In our Indian philosophy, where our thoughts have complete freedom, there are some systems and ideas which are completely acceptable to all from the Himalayas in the East to the sea coast in the South, which is an essential element for our Indian unity.

Now a days it is impossible to to take care of highly sensitive issues in which our ancestors were involved in teaching the way to lead a disciplined life with high values. We have been provided with a completely planned system to ensure that process of making our food and snacks is Hygienic followed by prohibited ingredients used in it, prohibition of adulteration in items used for worshipping, clothing material, Vaastu for housing, Pharmaceuticals, make up industry,ect Should be taken care by and within a management system and communities which are of strong values. Which can be followed with simplicity and ease. So that we can preserve our cultural beliefs which is based on our Indic traditions.

Sabhya Certification field

Food Industry

Providing certification we will ensure No Adulteration in the food and it’s preparation is according our Ritual and Indic Values and same process will be followed In its ingredients and Enterprises ( catering,hotels,restraunts,cafés,carts,ect)

Cosmetics and Medicines.

Ritualistic.

Buisness venture
